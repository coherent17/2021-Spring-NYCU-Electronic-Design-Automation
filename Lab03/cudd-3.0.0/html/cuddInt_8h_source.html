00130 ** must be even, so that they do not interfere with complementation bits.
00131 ** The least significant one is stored in Bits 3:1 of the f operand in the
00132 ** cache entry.  Bit 1 is always 1, so that we can differentiate
00133 ** three-operand operations from one- and two-operand operations.
00134 ** Therefore, the least significant digit is one of {2,6,a,e}.  The most
00135 ** significant digit occupies Bits 3:1 of the g operand in the cache
